About Us

The Raven Cafe draws its name from Edgar Allan Poe’s famous poem, “The Raven.” This literary connection sets the tone for an intriguing and mysterious ambiance.

The cafe is housed in a historic building dating back to the Civil War era, making it one of the oldest structures in Port Huron.
